How does Cydog Search Work?

What?

Privacy-focused and non-privacy search engines can track your search queries. You are banking on their integrity, as they have agreed not to compromise your data. We believe trust is essential but putting all that data in the hands of one company isn't smart. Where they prefer the cloud, we prefer local. Where they prefer centralized, we prefer decentralized.

Why?

We can't collect your data even if we wanted to. Cydog Search randomizes your queries and sends them to search engines that already exist. While our competitors sustain their money flow, we focus on innovation in the hopes you will pay for higher quality products when we monetize services in the future.

Who?

We focus on making your life easier. Our Search contains quick analytics so you can get information before you browse. We hope this empowers you.
